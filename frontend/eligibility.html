Eligibility Criteria for Platelet Donation

✅ Age

18 to 60 years old.

✅ Weight

At least 50 kg (110 lbs).

✅ Hemoglobin

Minimum 12.5 g/dL.

✅ General Health

Must be healthy and feeling well.

✅ No Recent Donation

Platelets: Wait at least 7 days. Whole blood: Wait 56 days.

❌ No Antibiotics

Should not be on antibiotics (unless for prevention).

❌ No Recent Infection

Must be symptom-free from cold or flu.

❌ No Recent Vaccination

Wait 14–28 days depending on the vaccine.

❌ No Recent Tattoos/Piercings

Wait at least 6 months.

❌ No Aspirin

Avoid aspirin 48 hours before donation.

❌ No Smoking/Alcohol

Avoid 24 hours before donation.

✅ Vein Quality

Must have good veins for platelet collection.
