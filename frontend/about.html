About Us

At PL8LETS, we believe that saving lives is a cause worth supporting. Our mission is to spread awareness about the importance of platelet donation and connect generous donors with patients in need.

Our platform allows you to easily register as a donor or find nearby donors when someone needs urgent help. We are committed to creating a compassionate and responsive community of everyday heroes.

With every platelet donated, you give someone a chance at life. Let’s come together to make a difference—one donation at a time.
